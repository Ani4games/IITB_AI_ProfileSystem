attendance — Only stores a two-column active/inactive flag per member (memberid + active). It's a system register used by whatever tool uploads daily swipe data, not a log. All actual attendance data comes from user_attendance.
restricted_holidays — These are optional Government of India holidays that staff choose to take. They don't affect mandatory day calculations and no RH leave type exists in the system — staff who take these appear as RL in the leaves table. Cross-referencing is possible but adds marginal value over the existing leave summary already shown.
formulae — Pure configuration data. It stores the weightage assigned to each appraisal field per review cycle — essentially the formula behind performance_score in performance_rating. Nothing to display to a user directly, and the computed scores are already shown in the appraisal section.
accessback — 118,038 rows of badge/swipe access logs. Investigated and ruled out because there is no cardno to member_id bridge anywhere in the profile table. The data exists but is not linkable to staff identities without additional infrastructure.
