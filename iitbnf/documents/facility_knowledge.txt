IITBNF FACILITY KNOWLEDGE BASE
================================

ABOUT THE FACILITY
------------------
IIT Bombay Nanofabrication Facility (IITBNF) is a state-of-the-art 
cleanroom facility located at IIT Bombay, Mumbai, India.
The facility provides fabrication and characterization services to 
researchers from IIT Bombay and external institutions under the 
National Programme on Micro and Smart Systems (NPMASS) and 
National Centre for Photovoltaic Research and Education (NCPRE).

FACILITY TYPE: Class 100 / Class 1000 Cleanroom
LOCATION: IIT Bombay Campus, Powai, Mumbai - 400076
OPERATING HOURS: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
WEBSITE: https://www.iitbnf.iitb.ac.in

TEAMS AND ROLES
---------------
IITBNF Staff: Core facility staff managing day-to-day lab operations,
equipment maintenance, and user support.

Faculty: Faculty members supervising research projects and student work
at the facility.

System Owner: Staff member assigned responsibility for a specific 
equipment — handles maintenance coordination and operational status.

HR Admin / HR Team: Manages staff attendance, leave, and personnel records.

IT Admin / IT Team: Manages the slotbooking system, user accounts,
and digital infrastructure.

Attendance Team: Responsible for uploading and verifying attendance records.

USER CATEGORIES
---------------
Ph.D: Doctoral researchers working towards a PhD degree.
M.Tech: Postgraduate students pursuing M.Tech degree.
M.Tech RA: M.Tech students on research assistantship.
B.Tech: Undergraduate students on project internship.
INUP: Visiting researchers under the INUP national nanofabrication programme.
PDF: Postdoctoral fellows conducting research.
Industry User: Industry users accessing facility for commercial R&D.
Project Staff: Staff supporting research operations on project basis.

ACCESS AND REGISTRATION
-----------------------
New users must register through the slotbooking portal.
Registration requires supervisor approval and course/project details.
Access is granted after registration review and approval.
Lab access expires based on registration period.
Access can be renewed by contacting facility staff.

EQUIPMENT BOOKING PROCESS
--------------------------
Step 1: Submit equipment usage request through slotbooking portal.
Step 2: Request reviewed and approved by system owner or faculty incharge.
Step 3: Once approved, slot booking is confirmed.
Step 4: User submits session report after equipment use.
Cancellations must be made in advance through the portal.

EQUIPMENT CATEGORIES
--------------------
Deposition: PECVD, LPCVD, Sputtering, Evaporation systems
Lithography: Spin coaters, Mask aligners, E-beam lithography
Etching: RIE, ICP etching systems, Wet bench stations  
Characterization: SEM, TEM, AFM, XRD, XPS, EDX systems
Thermal: Diffusion furnaces, RTA systems, Anneal ovens
Metrology: Profilometers, Ellipsometers, IV measurement

PUBLICATIONS AND PROJECTS
--------------------------
Users publish research papers associated with IITBNF.
Faculty projects are linked to user accounts.
Publications require approval before appearing in records.
Project categories include sponsored, consultancy, and student projects.

ATTENDANCE AND LEAVE POLICY
----------------------------
Mandatory attendance threshold: 75% of working days per year.
Leave types: Casual Leave (CL), Earned Leave (EL), Medical Leave (ML),
Restricted Leave (RL).
Attendance is recorded daily and tracked annually.
Staff below 75% threshold may require management review.

CONTACT AND SUPPORT
-------------------
For equipment issues: Contact the system owner for that equipment.
For access issues: Contact IT Admin through the portal.
For attendance queries: Contact HR Team.
For registration help: Contact facility staff.
